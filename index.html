Answering Your Sustainability Questions

Beyond Carbon provides instant answers to complex environmental impact questions

Country Consumption Footprint +

"What is Country X's carbon/material/water footprint by final demand category and by partner country?"

Our platform breaks down consumption-based footprints by sector and trading partner, revealing the true environmental cost of consumption.

Sector Efficiency Explorer +

"CO₂ per euro output by sector and country. Who is most/least efficient?"

Compare sectoral efficiency across countries to identify best practices and improvement opportunities.

Embodied Trade Flows +

"Which partner countries and sectors drive imported footprints of electronics/food/construction?"

Trace environmental impacts through global value chains to understand trade-embedded footprints.

Production vs Consumption +

"Gap between territorial emissions and consumption-based footprint."

Reveal the hidden environmental costs of consumption by comparing production-based to consumption-based accounts.

Jobs & Value Linkages +

"Jobs and VA embodied in exports/imports by sector."

Understand the employment and value-added implications of environmental policies and trade decisions.

Time Trends +

"How footprints and intensities changed 1995–latest."

Track environmental performance over time to measure progress and identify emerging trends.
