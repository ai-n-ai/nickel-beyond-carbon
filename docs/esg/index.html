ESG

Purpose

ESG (Environmental, Social, Governance) reporting provides transparency on sustainability and risk. This documentation focuses on the environmental dimension, supporting regulatory and voluntary disclosures.

Frameworks

Supported frameworks: GHG Protocol, CSRD, ISSB. Data and methods align with these standards for comparability and auditability.

What you get

Consistent, supply chain-aware environmental indicators for B2B reporting, with clear boundaries and units. Designed for integration into enterprise workflows.

Limitations: Not all ESG topics are covered. Social and governance aspects are out of scope.
