Supply chain

PBA vs CBA

Production-based (PBA) and consumption-based (CBA) accounting yield different supply chain insights. CBA includes upstream impacts embodied in purchased goods and services.

Embodied flows

We track embodied GHG, water, and materials across all supply chain tiers, using MRIO models for completeness.

Use cases

Common use cases: Scope 3 reporting, supplier engagement, and value chain benchmarking.

Limitations: Results depend on MRIO data quality and sectoral resolution.
