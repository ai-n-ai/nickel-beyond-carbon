MRIO

What MRIO provides

Multi-Regional Input-Output (MRIO) models quantify environmental impacts across global value chains, capturing direct and indirect flows.

Datasets we use

Primary sources: EXIOBASE, OECD ICIO/TiVA, WIOD. These provide harmonized, sector-resolved data for robust analysis.

Method overview

We use environmentally extended MRIO (EE-MRIO) methods, allocating impacts by final demand and sector.

Data quality & updates

Data are updated annually. Gaps and uncertainties are documented in the metadata.

Attribution & licensing

All results cite original data providers. Licensing terms are respected and detailed in the metadata.
