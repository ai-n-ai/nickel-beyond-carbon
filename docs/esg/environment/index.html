Environment

Indicators

Environmental indicators include GHG emissions, water use, and material footprint. All are supply chain-aware and suitable for B2B reporting.

Boundaries

We distinguish between production-based (PBA) and consumption-based (CBA) accounting. Final demand is the default boundary for CBA.

Units

All results are reported in SI units (e.g., kg CO₂e, m³ water). Units are shown with each indicator.

Common pitfalls

Beware of double counting and inconsistent boundaries when comparing datasets.
